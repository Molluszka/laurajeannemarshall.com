The City of Austin in recent years has had a population boom causing infamous traffic. One solution to decreasing the amount of vehicles throughout the city is to increase the usablity, appeal, and functionality of public transit via the mobile app.

Along with a small team, I followed a Lean UX methodology to create a modern, streamlined, & seamless tool for buying bus passes, planning trips, and integrating an interactive map.

This process included real-user interviews and an Heuristic analysis.
